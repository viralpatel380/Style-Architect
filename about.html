About us.

We are a creative ArchitectureStudio

We take this opportunity to introduce our selves as an Architect & interior designer, works under the name ‘‘Style Architects” since April 1984.
Our firm is based in Vadodara and expanded its activities in Gujarat. We have been engaged in the execution of several projects. Like residential, Industrial, Public buildings as well as interior design Projects.

We believe in honesty & transparency in clients dealing. we always try to create Harmony between human, materials & nature in our designing. We give similar importance and efforts to the small and big projects We always keep ourselves updated time to time with new concepts & new materials for enriching our services to you.
